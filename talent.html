How It Works: User Journeys

The Talent's Journey

1
Onboarding & Profile Setup

Sign up and build a skill-focused profile to showcase your creative abilities.

2
Showcase Work

Regularly post media (videos, audio, docs) and tag it with relevant skills to build your portfolio.

3
Grow Your Reach

Engage with the community and monitor your "Reach" score to increase visibility.

4
Get Hired

Connect with recruiters who discover your work and land your next creative project.

The Recruiter's Journey

1
Onboarding & Setup

Create a company or individual profile to start searching for talent.

2
Discover Talent

Use the powerful Discovery Engine with advanced filters to find the perfect skill set.

3
Evaluate & Outreach

Review portfolios of actual work and initiate conversations directly with talent.

4
Make a Hire

Discuss project details and hire top creative professionals through the platform.

Key App Features

Dual User Onboarding

Separate, tailored sign-up flows for "Talent" and "Recruiters".

Dynamic Talent Feed

A personalized, scrollable feed for creatives to network and find inspiration.

Skill-Tagged Portfolios

Profiles are live portfolios where all content is tagged with specific skills.

Powerful Discovery Engine

The core feature for recruiters with multi-faceted filtering capabilities.

Secure In-App Messaging

Direct and secure communication channel between talent and recruiters.

Reach Score & Notifications

Gamified visibility metric and an activity center to track engagement.
